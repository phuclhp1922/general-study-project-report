\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}
Our work introduce MORAv2, an adaptation of the original framework for wildfire to indoor fire emergencies. 
Via that transitioning, we successfully integrate a new layer of ``rescue'' people with a new factor of ``smoke''. 
Our implementation utilizes a three-phase lexicographic optimization that prioritize human life over property loss or deployment cost. 
To further enhance the practicality of our work, we develop an interactive web for users to provide their custom scenario and visualize
the optimal allocation in real time. 
Despite the potential of our work, we acknowledge its main limitation of deterministic nature via time-step simulation and 
simplified modeling. To further utilize our work, further work need to be done on stochastic modeling to address uncertainty and 
IoT sensor data for better optimization.
